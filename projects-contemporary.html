Contemporary

A bold exploration of contemporary living where clean architectural lines intersect with curated textures and thoughtful materiality. The Insidestory reimagines the modern home as a canvas for personal expression, blending old-world charm with an unmistakably current sensibility.

The living spaces unfold as a series of carefully composed vignettes, each anchoring a different mood within the home. The primary gathering area is anchored by a sweeping sectional in deep charcoal, set against walls finished in a warm lime plaster that catches the natural light filtering through floor-to-ceiling windows. The contrast between the coolness of polished concrete flooring and the warmth of walnut wood accents creates an atmosphere that feels both refined and inviting.

The kitchen becomes a stage for daily ritual — sleek cabinetry in matte sage green is paired with a Calacatta marble island, while brass hardware and pendant lights introduce a subtle gleam. Adjacent to the pantry, an open dining nook features a reclaimed teak table, deliberately weathered to tell its own story amidst the modernity surrounding it.

Ascending to the private quarters, the material palette softens. Bedrooms are wrapped in linen-textured wallpaper, with floating oak nightstands and custom headboards upholstered in bouclé. The primary suite opens onto a reading alcove bathed in south-facing light, where a vintage Moroccan rug anchors a pair of leather armchairs.

Throughout the home, art is not an afterthought but a structural element — oversized canvases share walls with curated gallery arrangements, while sculptural objects punctuate shelving units designed to frame rather than merely store. The result is an interior that feels like a living, breathing conversation between form, function, and the personalities that inhabit the space.
